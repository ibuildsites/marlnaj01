how it works

marlnaj is more than insurance. it's a fair, tech-first community that puts you in control.

you pay a flat fee

your monthly payment is split between a shared risk pool for community coverage and a personal savings account.

your fund grows

your personal repair fund builds over time. we reward safe driving with bonuses and rewards.

achieve independence

once your personal fund equals your car's value, you can opt out of the risk pool and rely solely on your own funds.

it just works.

our goal is to make sure you know it. we process all minor claims in under 48 hours. this isn't just a promise; it's our reputation.

no surprises. ever.

you'll always know exactly where your money is going. we keep our fees transparent so you feel like a valued member of a fair community, not just a customer.
